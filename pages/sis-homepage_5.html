01 — The Problem

Space is getting
crowded.

With thousands of active satellites and millions of debris fragments in orbit, the risk of collision is no longer theoretical. Current conjunction analysis tools produce massive false alert volumes — operators can't tell signal from noise.

SiS builds the uncertainty-aware forecasting layer that transforms raw trajectory data into credible, interpretable collision risk estimates.

02 — What We Build

Three principles.
One framework.

01

Safe

Probabilistic uncertainty quantification that never overconfidently dismisses collision risk. Our corrector framework propagates uncertainty faithfully from observation to decision — reducing false negatives that matter most.

02

Interpretable

Neural CDEs and predictor–corrector architectures grounded in physical dynamics. Operators understand what the model knows, what it doesn't, and why — bridging the gap between ML systems and mission-critical decisions.

03

Simple

Deployable on operational timelines. Clean interfaces for conjunction screening, risk scoring, and alert triage. Designed to reduce the cognitive burden on analysts — not add to it.

03 — Technology

The science
underneath.

Built on a rigorous foundation of continuous-time probabilistic modelling, orbital mechanics, and uncertainty propagation theory.

Core Architecture
Continuous-Time Probabilistic Corrector (CTPC)

Neural CDE-based framework that learns physics-residual corrections to SGP4/GMAT propagators while maintaining calibrated uncertainty intervals across the full forecast horizon.

Uncertainty Quantification
Fokker–Planck & SDE Propagation

Monte Carlo ensemble methods combined with Fokker–Planck-guided SDE dynamics for full covariance tracking — not just point estimates. PAC-Bayes bounds provide theoretical guarantees.

Conjunction Analysis
Probabilistic Collision Risk Inference

Direct probabilistic inference of Pc values with calibrated confidence intervals. Replaces hard thresholds with a continuous risk surface that informs operator decisions.

Data Pipeline
TLE / SGP4 Integration

End-to-end pipeline from raw TLE ingestion through GMAT propagation to ML corrector inference. Compatible with NASA CARA conjunction data message formats.
